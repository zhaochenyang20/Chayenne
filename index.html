About

I am a CS PhD student at the University of California, Los Angeles. I am supervised by Prof. Quanuan Gu and work closely with Prof. Ying Sheng.

I work on optimizing LLMs both on the system and algorithm level. You can check my Zhihu posts for what I am working on.

I obtained my Bachelor of Engineer Degree at Tsinghua University, Department of Computer Science and Technology.

I was fortunately supervised by Prof. Graham Neubig and Prof. Sherry Tongshuang Wu at Carnegie Mellon University during my undergrad years.
